Shah Rukh Khan

The World's Biggest Superstar

Shah Rukh Khan, after his team won IPL Season 7 in 2014
Shah Rukh Khan was born on, Nov 2, 1965 in New Delhi, India.
Khan is a pathan, and his father hails from Peshawar, Pakistan.
Khan studied in St. Columba's School, New Delhi.
Khan did his graduation from Hansraj college in New Delhi.
Khan also pursued masters in mass-media, but left in mid way.
Khan in his early years did theatre in Delhi, with Bary John.
From 1988 to 1992 he did television.
Since 1992 he is working in films, starting with "Deewana".
Khan is 51 years old, and is the biggest superstar in the world.
